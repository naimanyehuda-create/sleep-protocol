Posture Guide

Tap cards to reveal avoidance warnings.

1. Supine (Back)

Pillow: Flat placement directly under the head/neck. Never under shoulders.
Hands: Rest loosely at sides or folded gently on stomach.

Avoid

Do not sleep with your arms raised above your head. This compresses nerves and causes numbness.

2. Lateral (Side)

Pillow: Must fill the exact gap between ear and mattress to keep spine straight.
Hands: Extended slightly forward, loosely folded.

Avoid

Do not jam your hand under the pillow (cuts circulation). Avoid curling into a tight fetal position.

Sleep Modifiers

Reference data from Advanced Protocol Guidelines.

Military Shutdown Steps

Step 1: Facial & Shoulder Drop. Close your eyes, slacken your jaw, and drop your shoulders completely into the mattress.
Step 5: Arm & Leg Shutdown. Let your arms go numb. Then your thighs, calves, and feet.

Optimal Temperature

Keep your room between 65-68°F (18-20°C). A physical drop in your core body temperature is biologically required to initiate the sleep cycle.

The 20-Minute Rule

If you complete the breathing method and are still awake after 20 minutes, leave the bed. Read a book in dim light until you feel tired, then return.

Audio Distraction

If silence causes your brain to overthink, use a fan or white noise machine. If using AirPods for lectures, always use a 20-minute sleep timer and remove them before falling asleep to protect ear health.

Stimulant Curfews

Vaping introduces nicotine. Your absolute final vape must be at 9:30 PM (or immediately post-class) to give your heart rate 2 hours to normalize.
